### What this is

**Still Relevant** is a practical, honest guide for working professionals who feel the ground shifting — and want to know exactly where to stand.

It's not a tech book. It's not a motivational poster. It doesn't tell you to "just learn to code" or pretend everything is fine.

It's the book a smart, straight-talking friend would hand you if they actually knew the landscape — someone who'd tell you what's real, what's noise, and what to do right now. This guide covers everything from FOBO (the Fear of Becoming Obsolete — the anxiety most professionals feel but can't name) to practical day-by-day strategies for working alongside AI. Delivered as a PDF ebook + fillable toolkit.

---

### What's inside

**The ebook (15,000–20,000 words) covers:**

- **The Feeling Nobody's Talking About** — naming the anxiety, validating it, and explaining why it's happening to everyone right now
- **What's Actually Happening** — cutting through doom and hype to show you what AI can and can't do *right now*, with historical context that reframes everything
- **The Things AI Will Never Have** — lived experience, emotional intelligence, genuine creative vision, moral judgment, and the ability to *care*
- **Finding Your Unique Edge** — a framework for identifying your specific, irreplaceable strengths
- **AI as Your Partner** — the mental model shift that changes everything, with real examples by profession
- **Your First 7 Days with AI** — a day-by-day action guide to start using AI *for you*, not against you
- **Conversations That Matter** — what to say to your boss, your team, and yourself
- **The New Career Advantage** — the skills that are *rising* in value because of AI, and how to position yourself
- **Beyond Survival** — from fear to curiosity to confidence to power. The full arc.
- **A Letter to You** — a direct, personal close that sends you back into your work differently

**Every chapter ends with a Key Takeaway and one concrete Action Step.**

---

**The companion toolkit (7 pieces) includes:**

1. **The Human Edge Audit** — a guided worksheet to identify exactly what makes you irreplaceable
2. **AI Partner Plan** — a fill-in template for integrating AI into your specific role, your specific way
3. **First 7 Days Checklist** — the day-by-day guide from Chapter 6 in printable format
4. **Workplace Conversation Scripts** — copy-paste templates for real conversations with your boss, your team, and yourself
5. **Skills Value Map** — a visual tool showing what's rising vs. declining in your field
6. **The Reframe Journal** — 5 days of prompts to shift from fear to clarity
7. **AI Tool Starter Kit** — curated beginner-friendly AI tools by profession, with notes on what each is actually useful for

### Who this is for

Working professionals aged 25–45 who:
- Have built a real career and feel uncertain about what AI means for it
- Are not technical and don't want to be
- Want honest answers, not hype or doom
- Need practical steps, not inspiration-poster reassurance

This is **not** for people who want to build AI systems, learn to code, or become "prompt engineers." This is for the marketing manager, the teacher, the operations lead, the writer, the healthcare worker, the creative, the administrator — the people who make organizations actually function, and who deserve a straight answer about their future.
